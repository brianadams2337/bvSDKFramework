Helpful Tips
Tips for writing reviews:
  • Focus on the product and your experience with it
  • Aim for clear and concise language
  • Be specific. Whats great about it? Whats not so great?

Avoid:

  • Profanity or spiteful remarks
  • Obscene or distasteful content
  • E-mail addresses, phone numbers and URLs
  • Information about other companies and Web sites
  • Telling customers to shop elsewhere
Media guidelines
Media guidelines
  • Up to 2 photos, 5MB each
  • Focus on the product and how you use it
  • All photos must be your own
  • No nudity or obscene content
  • Inappropriate photos will cause your entire review to be rejected
